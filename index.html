Sustainable Kamloops

Kamloops has many local companies, businesses and people that can help you on your green living journey.

Rare Birds housing co-op is a great local example of sustainable living.

The City of Kamloops holds an annual Green Living Expo, there is a comprehensive Environmental & Sustainability Plan and you can even subscribe to their quarterly Sustainable Kamloops eNewsletter.

Start Your Sustainable Journey

  1. Reduce your plastic consumption
  2. Buy local
  3. Take public transit or carpool
